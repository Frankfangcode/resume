關於我About

我的學習歷程從企管到統計，再到資管——不是迷失方向，而是每一步都在疊加視角。我懂程式、懂數據、懂商業、懂金融，最終落腳在「如何用科技解決真實問題」。My path from Business Administration to Statistics to Information Management wasn't wandering — it was deliberately stacking lenses. I understand code, data, business, and finance, all converging on one question: how do you use technology to solve real problems?

「科技不只是技術，而是一種解決問題、創造新機會的工具。」 "Technology isn't just code — it's a tool for solving real problems and creating new possibilities."

我創辦了政大金融科技創新實驗室，邀請政大金融科技研究中心主任王儷玲擔任指導老師；與EMBA學長共同開發Alpha Pipeline十層量化投資系統；帶著沈錡大使獎學金赴日本九州實地考察台積電供應鏈；在2026全國大專國際經貿競賽以供應鏈重組研究拿下全國第二。I founded NCCU's FinTech Innovation Lab, secured Prof. Wang Li-Ling as advisor. Co-developed Alpha Pipeline with EMBA alumni. Flew to Kyushu on scholarship to investigate TSMC's supply chain. Won National 2nd at the 2026 Int'l Trade Competition with supply chain research.

但讓我最難忘的，是在深坑山上遇到那位90歲的阿嬤。她是整座山的活記憶庫。我們把她的聲音用AI留下來，把她的樣貌用3D印出來，讓記憶公仔繼續說話——在薪燼之前。But what stays with me most: meeting a 90-year-old grandma on a mountain in Shenkeng. She was the living memory of the whole hill. We captured her voice with AI, 3D-printed her likeness, so the memory doll keeps talking — before the fire goes out.

我也游泳，代表政大參加全國大專運動會，自由式接力全國第七。泳道和程式碼教我同樣的事：只要持續推進，就能突破。I also swim — representing NCCU at the National University Games, 7th in freestyle relay. The lane and the codebase teach the same thing: keep moving forward, and you'll break through.
